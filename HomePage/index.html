Welcome to Cyber Threat Intelligence 101!

What is Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI)?

CTI is the process of gathering and analyzing information about cyber threats. It helps organizations understand the "who," "what," "where," "when," "why," and "how" of cyber attacks. This knowledge allows organizations to proactively defend their networks and data.

Why is CTI Important?

Cyber threats are constantly evolving, so staying informed is crucial. By understanding attacker tactics, you can:

  • Strengthen your defenses: Identify vulnerabilities and prioritize security measures to address the most relevant threats.
  • Improve threat detection: Recognize suspicious activity and respond to potential attacks faster.
  • Predict future attacks: Analyze past tactics to anticipate future attack methods and implement pre-emptive countermeasures.
